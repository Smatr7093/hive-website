3. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account Information: Full name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, and password.

Profile Information: Profile photographs, bio, listed skills and activities, availability, and session rates voluntarily provided by you.

Identity Verification Data: Government-issued identity documents for KYC verification purposes. This data is processed solely for fraud prevention, age verification, and preventing duplicate accounts. This data is stored with industry-standard encryption and is deleted when you delete your account.

Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, session duration, device type, browser type, operating system, IP address, and referral source.

Communication Data: Content of messages exchanged through the Platform's chat system (subject to our content moderation policy), booking requests, and support correspondence.

Payment Information: Transaction identifiers, payment method type, billing amount, payment status, UTR/reference numbers, optional payment proof screenshots, and manual UPI verification notes. Full bank credentials are never stored on our servers. The checkout warning asks users to pay only through official Hive checkout payment gateway.

Subscription Data: Plan type, purchase history, and subscription status.

Location Data: Approximate location inferred from your IP address. We do not collect precise GPS location unless you explicitly grant permission.

Device and Technical Data: Push notification tokens, device identifiers, and app usage analytics.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

To provide and operate the Platform: Creating and managing your account, enabling bookings and messaging, processing payments, and delivering the features you access.

Identity and age verification: Confirming that users are adults (18 years or older) and that profiles are genuine, using KYC documents.

Safety and fraud prevention: Detecting, investigating, and preventing fraudulent, abusive, or illegal activity on the Platform.

Content moderation: Automatically and manually reviewing messages, media, and profile content to enforce our community guidelines and prevent prohibited conduct. Automated moderation includes keyword-based text filtering, AI-based content analysis. Flagged content may be reviewed by authorized team members.

Communications: Sending transactional emails (booking confirmations, payment receipts), service updates, security alerts, and, where you have opted in, promotional notifications.

Platform improvement: Analyzing usage patterns, troubleshooting technical issues, and developing new features.

Legal compliance: Meeting obligations imposed by Indian law, court orders, or regulatory authorities, and enforcing our Terms & Conditions.

Customer support: Responding to your queries, resolving disputes, and providing technical assistance.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

These vendors are contractually obligated to protect your data and may only use it to perform services on our behalf.

For legal purposes: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, regulation, court order, or government authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect rights, property, or safety of the Platform, our users, or the public.

In business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or a prominent notice on the Platform before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

With your consent: We may share your data in other circumstances with your explicit prior consent.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide the Services.

Account deletion by user: If you delete your account through account settings, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by applicable law (for example, financial records which must be retained for a minimum period under Indian tax law), or where retention is necessary for fraud prevention, security investigations, or unresolved disputes.

Deletion record: When an account is deleted (whether by you, by us for policy violations), we retain a limited administrative record containing your email address, name, account type, last active date, and the date and reason for deletion. This record is retained for accounting, fraud prevention, and regulatory compliance purposes and does not include your profile content, chat history, media, or any other personal data.

Message content exchanged through the Platform may be retained for a period necessary to comply with our legal obligations and to investigate reported violations. Anonymized and aggregated data that cannot reasonably identify you may be retained indefinitely for analytical purposes
